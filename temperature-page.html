Temperature by Location Latitude


This plot compares each city in the dataset's temperature to it's latitude. The plot shows that the temperature increases the closer a city is to the equator (latitude 0) and decreases the further the city is from the equator. It also shows that the dataset includes alot more northern hemisphere cities than southern hemisphere cities. This makes sense because the northern hemisphere has more land mass than the southern hemisphere and therefore likely more cities.
